Cricket

Cricket is a globally popular sport known for its strategic gameplay, skilled athleticism, and rich traditions. It is played between two teams, each consisting of eleven players, on a circular field with a rectangular 22-yard pitch in the center.

The primary objective in cricket is to score runs by hitting the ball with a bat and running between the wickets or hitting boundaries. The opposing team tries to dismiss the batsmen and limit the number of runs scored. Cricket matches can take various formats, including Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) matches.

Cricket has a rich history and is deeply ingrained in the cultures of many countries, especially in countries like India, England, Australia, and Pakistan. International competitions, such as the ICC Cricket World Cup, bring together teams from around the world to compete for cricket's most prestigious title.

The sport has evolved over the years, introducing new formats, technologies like Decision Review System (DRS), and captivating fans with thrilling moments, such as sixes, wickets, and close finishes. Cricket also fosters a sense of sportsmanship, teamwork, and camaraderie among players.
